POLICY NAME: Niva Bupa ReAssure 2.0 Platinum Plus Plan
INSURER: Niva Bupa Health Insurance Company Ltd.
PREMIUM: Rs. 22,000 per year
COVER AMOUNT: Rs. 10,00,000 (10 Lakhs)

WAITING PERIODS
Initial waiting period: 30 days except accidents.
Diabetes: Covered from Day 1 with no waiting period under Platinum Plus.
Hypertension: Covered from Day 1 with no waiting period.
Asthma: Covered from Day 1. Cardiac conditions: 12 months.
Pre-existing disease general waiting period: 24 months, reduced to 12 months for renewals.

INCLUSIONS
Unlimited restoration of sum insured once per year for unrelated illnesses.
No room rent sub-limit, any room category covered fully.
Pre-hospitalization 60 days. Post-hospitalization 180 days.
OPD up to Rs. 20,000 per year with digital consultation included.
Mental health inpatient and outpatient up to Rs. 1,00,000.
Maternity benefit up to Rs. 1,00,000 after 24 months. International emergency up to USD 25,000.
Annual comprehensive health check-up every year. Robotic surgery fully covered.

EXCLUSIONS
Cosmetic procedures. Self-inflicted harm. Non-emergency international treatment.
Infertility beyond maternity limit. Experimental unapproved treatments.

SUB-LIMITS AND CO-PAY
No room rent sub-limit. No co-pay in network hospitals at any age.
5 percent co-pay only for non-network hospital claims.
No sub-limits on modern treatments or robotic surgery.
Cashless at 10,000 plus hospitals with 30-minute pre-auth guarantee.

SUITABILITY
Best for age 30 to 55 with income above 15 lakhs in Metro cities.
Ideal for Diabetes, Hypertension, Asthma patients due to Day 1 coverage with no waiting period.
Athletes and Active lifestyle individuals benefit from OPD and mental health coverage.
Not cost-effective for income under 8 lakhs due to high Rs. 22,000 annual premium.
Suitability Score: 92 out of 100 for high-income Metro residents with pre-existing conditions.
