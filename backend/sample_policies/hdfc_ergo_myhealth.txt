POLICY NAME: HDFC ERGO My Health Suraksha Silver Smart Plan
INSURER: HDFC ERGO General Insurance Company Ltd.
PREMIUM: Rs. 7,200 per year
COVER AMOUNT: Rs. 3,00,000 (3 Lakhs)

WAITING PERIODS
Initial waiting period: 30 days. Pre-existing disease waiting period: 48 months.
Cardiac conditions: 48 months with 15 percent premium loading.
Asthma: 36 months waiting period. Specific illnesses like piles and sinusitis: 24 months.

INCLUSIONS
Inpatient treatment up to sum insured. Pre-hospitalization 30 days. Post-hospitalization 60 days.
Daycare: 144 listed procedures. Ambulance up to Rs. 1500.
AYUSH up to Rs. 15,000. Domiciliary treatment covered. Health check-up once every 3 years.

EXCLUSIONS
Dental unless hospitalization required. Cosmetic treatments. Obesity treatment.
Maternity and newborn expenses not covered. Pre-existing conditions during waiting period.

SUB-LIMITS AND CO-PAY
Room rent 1 percent of sum insured per day maximum Rs. 3000. ICU Rs. 6000 per day.
Co-pay: 20 percent for non-network hospitals. No co-pay for network cashless below 60 years.
Cataract Rs. 25,000 per eye. Cashless at 10,000 plus network hospitals.

SUITABILITY
Best for age 20 to 45 with income under 8 lakhs in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities.
Ideal for individuals without pre-existing conditions due to long 48-month waiting period.
Sedentary lifestyle lower income bracket benefit most from affordable premium.
Not suitable for Diabetes, Hypertension or Cardiac patients due to very long waiting periods.
Suitability Score: 85 out of 100 for young healthy Tier-3 city residents under 8L income.
